Why a website still matters

Social media is borrowed space. An algorithm decides who sees your shop today, and the platform can change the rules tomorrow — a page can get buried, restricted, or lost in a redesign overnight.

A website is the one corner of the internet that's actually yours. It works while you sleep, it's the first thing that shows up when someone searches your name, and it makes a small business look like it means business.
